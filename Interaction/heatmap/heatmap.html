Interaction in D3 - Heatmap Example

Move your mouse and click on squares to see the interactions.

Borders on divs are to make the layout clearer. Notice how the tooltip div attempts to stay within its enclosing div, but can't quite do it when the mouse is on a square in the rightmost column.

This is example is based on an example from d3-graph-gallery.org but has been updated to use D3 version 5 and modified in several ways.
